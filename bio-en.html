ABOUT ME

Pilot. Creative. Storyteller.

My name is Roman Scholz — I'm a certified drone pilot from Wuppertal and have been in the air since 2013. What began as a passion for technology and moving images has grown into a professional craft that clients like Canyon Bicycles, Siemens, and Mercedes-Benz have come to rely on.

To me, FPV is about more than speed: it's about precision, timing, and the ability to tell a story through movement. Every turn, every flythrough, every angle is deliberately chosen — to capture the audience's attention and show what's in front of the camera in a completely new light.

Alongside classic outdoor footage, I specialize in cine FPV — heavy camera systems mounted on a highly stable FPV frame, combining cinema-grade quality with the dynamics of FPV flying.

I mainly work across Germany. For larger projects I'm also available internationally.
